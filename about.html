About me

Hi! I'm runarcn (he/him), a music student and among other things free time FOSS advocate. I currently live near Trondheim, Norway. In my free time I enjoy playing chess, programming, reading and watching series. Sometimes I also write blogs, both here and on Substack.


I made this website as a passion project to learn more about HTML, CSS and git. If you want to view the source code there's a link at the bottom of this page. Naturally, everything is free software and licensed under the GPL.


While a webpage is useful for a lot of stuff, it's not the best way for someone to get to know me. Therefore, if you want to ask me anything, have a chat or whatever, head over to my links page and shoot me a DM wherever you like. You'll also find my Lichess page if you want to play chess.
